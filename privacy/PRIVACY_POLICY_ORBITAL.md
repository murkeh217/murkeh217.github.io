## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Data Collected by the App

We do not directly collect or store personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

However, the app uses third-party advertising services that may collect certain information automatically.

---

### 1.2 Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device using Unity PlayerPrefs:

* High score
* Coin balance
* Purchased/unlocked cosmetic items
* Theme preferences

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

---

### 1.3 Third-Party Data Collection (Unity Ads)

Orbital uses Unity Ads to display advertisements. Unity Ads may automatically collect and process certain data, including:

* Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID)
* IP address
* Device type and operating system
* App activity (ad views, interactions, session data)
* Approximate location (derived from IP address)
* Diagnostics data (crash and performance data)

This data is used for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention.

For more details, please review Unity’s Privacy Policy:
https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy
