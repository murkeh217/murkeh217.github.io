## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 No Personal Data Collected by Us

Orbital does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or home address. We have no accounts, no sign-in system, and no social login features.

### 1.2 Local Storage Only

The app stores the following data **locally on your device** using Unity's `PlayerPrefs` system:

- High score
- Coin balance
- Purchased/unlocked cosmetic items
- Theme randomization preference (on/off)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server. You can clear it at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's local data in your device settings.

### 1.3 Unity Ads (Third-Party)

Orbital uses **Unity Ads** (provided by Unity Technologies) to serve banner, interstitial, and optional rewarded video ads. Unity Ads may automatically collect certain information for ad personalization, performance, and fraud prevention. This may include:

- Device identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID)
- IP address
- Device type and operating system version
- App usage data (e.g., when an ad is shown or interacted with)
- General location data (not precise GPS)

Unity Ads operates under its own privacy policy. For full details, see:  
🔗 [Unity Privacy Policy](https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy)

If you wish to opt out of interest-based advertising on Android, you can enable **"Opt out of Ads Personalization"** in your device's **Settings → Google → Ads** menu.

## 2. How We Use Information

- **Local data** (scores, coins, purchases) is used solely to persist your in-game progress and personalization.
- **Ad data** collected by Unity Ads is used to serve and measure advertisements, including personalized ads where permitted by your device settings.

---

## 3. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. The only third-party service integrated into the app is Unity Ads, which processes data as described in Section 1.3 above.

---

## 4. Data Retention and Deletion

- **Local data** persists on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data via system settings. No server-side copy exists.
- **Unity Ads data** is retained per Unity's own privacy policy. We have no access to or control over data collected by Unity Ads.

To delete all data associated with the app:
1. Uninstall Orbital from your device, or
2. Go to **Settings → Apps → Orbital → Storage → Clear Data**

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

Orbital is a general-audience arcade game and is **not directed at children under the age of 13**. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal data through this app, please contact us so we can arrange for its deletion.

If you are under 13, do not use this app.
