Bodyweight Workout Schedule

Equipment-free program focusing on stamina, speed, strength, endurance, mobility, and control.

Day Focus Workout (Warm-up • Main • Cooldown)
Day 1 — Power & Strength
Explosive full-body
 Burst / Strength
Warm-up: Arm circles, hip swings, jog 5 min
  • Explosive Pushups (clap/fast) 4×10
  • Dive Bomber Pushups 3×10 • Pike Pushups 3×8
  • Jump Squats 4×12 • Forward Lunges 3×12/leg
  • Plank→Pushup 3×10 • Wall Sit 3×45s
Cooldown: Hamstring stretch + 3 min deep breathing
Day 2 — Endurance & Core
High volume & breath work
 Stamina / Recovery
Warm-up: Jumping jacks or skipping 3–5 min
  • (4 rounds) Pushups 20 • Wide Pushups 15
  • Bodyweight Squats 30 • Lunges 20 • Mountain Climbers 30s
  • Flutter Kicks 30s • Plank 1:00
Finisher: 2 min paced breathing (4s inhale, 6s exhale)
Day 3 — Mobility & Control
Isometrics, balance & flow
 Flexibility / Control
Warm-up: Slow dynamic stretches
  • Isometric Pushup Hold (bottom) 30s×3
  • Wall Sit 60s×3 • Hollow Hold 30s×3
  • Horse Stance 45s×3 • Slow 5s-down squats ×10
  • Controlled kicks & shadowboxing (technique + breath)
Cooldown: Static stretches / yoga 8–10 min
Day 4 — Speed & Reflex
Plyo + reaction
 Speed / Agility
Warm-up: High knees & arm swings 3 min
  • Plyo Pushups 4×8 • Quick Burpees 4×10
  • Jump Lunges 3×12 • Squat Jumps 3×10
  • Sprint-in-place 20s ×4 • Fast shadowboxing rounds (1 min)
  • Side plank punch 30s/side ×3
Cooldown: Gentle mobility + breath work
Day 5 — Strength + Stamina Circuit
Combine intensity & volume
 Hybrid / Conditioning
Warm-up: Joint mobility + 3 min light jog
  • 3 rounds — 1 min rest between rounds:
  • • Pushups 15 • Narrow Pushups 10 • Jump Squats 15
  • • Reverse Lunges 10/leg • Burpees 10 • Plank Shoulder Taps 20
  • • Horse Stance 30s
Finisher: Deep squat hold 1min + plank 1min
Day 6 — Flexibility, Martial Flow & Core
Active recovery & technique
 Mobility / Skill
Warm-up: 5-min slow movement flow
  • Cat–Cow ×10 • Downward Dog→Cobra ×10
  • Lunges with twist 10/side • Deep squat stretch 1min
  • Slow kicks (balance) 10/leg • Shadowboxing slow flow 5×2min
  • Hollow hold 30s×3 • Side plank 30s/side×3
Cooldown: Yoga poses (pigeon, butterfly, child) 5–8 min
Day 7 — Rest / Active Recovery
Light movement
 Recovery
Optional: Light walk, gentle yoga, 5 min breath meditation, or slow shadowboxing for mobility and flow.
