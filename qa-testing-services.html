QA Testing Services

Techprof Ltd, offer comprehensive manual and automated software testing services. Our goal is to help you deliver software of high quality by identifying and eliminating bugs, errors, and other issues.

Our thorough QA testing approach ensures complete test coverage at every stage of the software development process. To achieve this, we combine the reliability of manual testing with the efficiency of automation testing.

Applications of Software Testing

Product Improvement

Throughout the SDLC level

Quality Check:

We provide software testing that assesses various aspects of software including:

  • Functionality
  • Reliability
  • Usability
  • Efficiency
  • Maintenance
  • Portability

Testing Stages:

Our testing services encompass different levels and methods to ensure quality assurance in IT software testing. The major levels are functional testing and non-functional testing.

Functional Testing:

Our services combine effective testing processes, skilled technology experts, and domain knowledge to ensure flawless product delivery. We also offer end-to-end manual testing services tailored to meet your functional testing requirements.

Steps used by our expert for functionality testing

  • Determining the intended functionality of the application.
  • Developing test data based on the application's specifications.
  • Comparing the application's output with the test data and specifications.
  • Writing and executing test cases.
  • Comparing the actual results with the expected results based on the executed test cases.

Application Testing:

Application testing provides you the chance to reduce costs and incorporate outsourcing into your operation with lower risk. Our team provides single-testing applications or comprehensive IT system testing. We ensure the predictability of application performance through the use of the industry-standard testing methodology. We offer a single point of resource responsibility for testing and governance.

Usability Testing:

Our usability experts can assist you in improving the user experience of your application. They will develop a usability test strategy based on factors such as user types, demographics, and key business scenarios. Our team will select suitable users, create survey questions, and provide guidance to the participants. We will monitor user behavior in real-time and conduct interviews to gain deeper insights into the application's navigation and other features.

Performance Testing:

Our team helps predict how applications will behave and perform in a simulated, real-life environment. This proactive approach protects you from potential issues that can frustrate customers and impact profits. We ensure that applications are responsive and reliable, even during peak load days. Our testing also ensures that applications meet contractual obligations and performance service level agreements (SLAs). This type of testing focuses on identifying performance issues rather than detecting software bugs.
